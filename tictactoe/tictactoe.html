🎮 How to Play
✏️
Set Player Names
Click the pencil icon next to a player to rename them. Press Enter or click the icon again to confirm.
🔄
Take Turns
Player X always goes first. Click any empty cell to place your symbol. Players alternate turns.
🏆
Win the Game
Get 3 of your symbols in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — before your opponent does.
⏱️
12-Second Timer
Each player has 12 seconds per turn. If time runs out, the turn is automatically skipped. Use ⏸ Pause to freeze the clock.
🕹️
Extra Buttons
↩ Undo takes back the last move. 🤖 AI Move plays the perfect move for the current player. ✦ New Game resets the board without clearing scores.
💡 Tip: The AI uses minimax — it never makes a mistake. Try beating it!
